Meghamalai

Meghamalai, commonly known as the High Wavy Mountains, is a mountain range located in the Western Ghats in the Theni district near Kumily, Tamil Nadu. It is dotted with cardamom plantations and tea plantations.

Tourist Attraction

  • Tea estates
  • Manalaru Dam
  • Vellimali
  • Falls
  • Murugan Temple
  • View of Cumbum Valley
